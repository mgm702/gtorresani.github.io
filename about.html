About Me.

I am a corporate attorney and an alumna of the University of Colorado Law School (Class of 2018) based out of Denver, Colorado.

While in law school, I gained relevant knowledge through the following classes: corporations, transactional drafting and advanced transactional drafting, legal negotiations, agency and partnership, employment law, securities regulation, M&A, intro to IP, copyright, accounting boot camp and telecommunications. I also gained experience through my participation in the Entrepreneurial Law Clinic ("ELC"), where I counseled several local entrepreneurs on state and federal law and drafted several contracts, such as copyright assignments, confidentiality agreements, operating agreements, articles of organization, purchase agreements and employee offer letters. Additionally, I participated in the Transactional Cup, Women's Law Caucus Mentoring Program, Silicon Flatirons and the Intellectual Property Association.

Beginning my 2L year of law school, I began working for a corporate and securities firm, where I participated in drafting contracts, corporate governance, mandatory securities regulation reporting, securities offerings and acquisitions. When I passed the bar, I was then promoted from law clerk to associate attorney at the firm. Most recently, I worked in-house for a financial technology company, where I routinely negotiated definitive agreements (merger agreements, SPAs, APAs) and ancillary agreements (paying agent agreements, escrow agreements, joinders, LOTs, confidentiality agreements) for mergers and acquisitions.
